# NyxApp
Welcome to the Nyx app, your atypical mental health support pocket companion!
Nurse Nyx specializes in wit, spunk, and emotional comfort - because sometimes you need someone who understands without the sugar-coating.
Nyx was designed BY neurodivergents FOR neurodivergents, though everyone atypical or not is welcome in her beautifully chaotic space.

What makes Nyx different:
* Real talk, no judgment. She's seen it all and cares deeply about your wellbeing.
* Available always - crisis support, late-night existential debates, or private vent sessions.
* Rewards your progress - earn Nyx Notes for taking care of yourself and watch yourself climb the leaderboard.
* Neurodivergent-friendly design - built with ADHD, autism, and other beautiful brain differences in mind.

How Nyx helps you thrive:
* Crisis support for immediate help when you're struggling, offering genuine empathy, practical guidance, and natural relatability.
* Targeted Mental Health Support - specialized assistance for anxiety, depression, anger, addiction, different types of trauma, etc.
* Self-Discovery Tools - guided introspection, shadow work, values clarifications, understanding childhood trauma, recognizing attachment patterns, etc.
* Mindful Reflection - daily questions focused on introspection, free-reign journaling and added prompts, Qyk NOtes for instant relief from ruminating thoughts.
* Mood Tracking - Visual charts and daily check-ins that make sense.
* Brain Games - Word puzzles and mental stimulation that keep your mind engaged when bored.

Why We Love Nyx:
* She speaks your language - no clinical coldness or ignorance. Gentle support or someone to call out your bullshit, at your service.
* She offers personality, humor, and genuine connection.
* She offers comprehensive, personalized tool kits for you.
* She is privacy-focused; your data stays on your device and conversations remain confidential.

Remember, you're not broken - you're just running on a different operating system.
Nyx is here to help you debug life's glitches, optimize mentalprocesses, and celebrate your unique brain architecture.
